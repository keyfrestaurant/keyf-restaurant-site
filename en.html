ABOUT

Where Time Slows Down and Pleasure Begins

Right on the waterfront where sailboats drift in and the Aegean breeze carries the salt of the sea, Key-f is the kind of place you find and never want to leave. From a late morning coffee to a long dinner watching the sun melt into the water — we're here for all of it.

☀️

Morning at the Marina

We open at 10 and ease into the day from there. Quality, additive-free ingredients, warm çay and an uninterrupted view of the marina — a combination that's hard to beat.

🍽️

Lunch Through Dinner

Light, fresh midday dishes give way to carefully crafted evening plates and meze spreads. Every meal is prepared with the same attention to quality and the same unhurried pace.

⚓

Marina Atmosphere

Boats coming and going, the shimmer of the sea, sunset colours reflecting on the water. Key-f isn't just somewhere to eat — it's somewhere to slow down and be present.

🤝

Special Occasions & Groups

Family gatherings, anniversaries, group dinners or private celebrations — we're happy to accommodate. Reach out via WhatsApp and we'll arrange everything together.

WHY KEY-F?

The Best Restaurant in Didim

With its marina atmosphere, thoughtfully prepared dishes and peaceful setting, Key-f is more than just a restaurant — it's a place to slow down and enjoy the moment. Perfect for families, couples and groups looking for a relaxed yet refined dining experience on the Turkish Aegean coast.
